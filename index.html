Walking With People Through Grief

A church-based bereavement care system designed to provide structured, compassionate, and long‑term support for individuals and families experiencing loss.

“The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit.”

— Psalm 34:18

About the Program

The Care After Loss system helps churches move from short-term funeral support to ongoing, intentional care. By understanding age differences and stages of grief, the church provides personalized emotional, spiritual, and community-based support.

Our Care Model

Immediate Care

Prayer, presence, and practical support during early loss.

Community Support

Mentorship, visits, and fellowship inclusion.

Structured Follow‑Up

Check-ins at regular intervals after both during the healing period and after.

Healing Support

Support groups, counseling referrals, and spiritual guidance.
