Walking With People Through Grief

Welcome to our church-based bereavement care system designed to provide structured, compassionate, and long‑term support for both during and after the grieving process for individuals and families experiencing loss.

“The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit.”

— Psalm 34:18

About the Program

The Care After Loss system helps churches move from short-term funeral support to ongoing, intentional care. By understanding age differences and stages of grief, the church provides personalized emotional, spiritual, and community-based support.

Our Care Model

Immediate Care

We offer prayer sessions, church visits and practical support during early loss stages to help families feel cared for and supported in their time of need.

Community Support

We provide mentorship sessions, church visits and fellowship inclusion to help the bereaved feel supported and connected to the church family.

Structured Follow‑Up

Check-ins at regular intervals both during and after the healing period to ensure continued support and care.

Healing Support

We provide support groups, therapy sessions, counseling referrals and spiritual guidance through regular bible study, prayer and notifications for when sermons involve bereaved individuals.
